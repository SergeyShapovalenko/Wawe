About Surfing in the Canaries

The Canary Islands are often referred to as the European Hawaiis – it's the perfect destination for surfing enthusiasts: stable warm winds year-round, sandy beaches, kilometers of untouched spaces, and crystal-clear waters. Fuerteventura, the second-largest island in the archipelago, becomes a true mecca for surfers.

Indeed, this is the best place in Spain to receive surfing lessons. Apart from the massive Atlantic waves that roll in during the winter and fall, Fuerteventura boasts numerous shallow, tranquil lagoons that are perfect for teaching novice surfers.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available year-round, thanks to the consistent waves and favorable weather conditions. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, you'll find waves suited to your skill level. It's no coincidence that this place is considered the best for learning to surf and advancing to the next level with ease.

Among its many advantages, it's worth mentioning that Fuerteventura boasts the longest beaches in the Canary Islands. As a result, overcrowding by tourists is virtually unheard of here. You'll only come across like-minded individuals who share your passion for learning to surf.

Whether you're just curious about the world of surfing or you've already experienced the thrill of riding the waves and want to take your skills to the next level, our surfing school can provide lessons tailored to your skill level.

Four hours of engaging daily lessons will help you confidently conquer the waves and make you feel like a true surfer.

The main advantages of our school include high-quality teaching and lesson organization, safety (all our clients are insured with Allianz), excellent equipment and gear (provided for the entire course, including soft, safe boards for beginners).

First, we assess your skill level and physical fitness, and only then do we create a training program. We can assure you that after completing the training, you will not only have a fantastic experience but will also advance to a new skill level.
